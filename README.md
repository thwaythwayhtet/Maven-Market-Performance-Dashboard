## 📈 Maven Market Performance Dashboard

✨ <u>About this project</u>

**1. Objective** <br>
The objective of this project was to provide a comprehensive view of business performance across key dimensions, including sales, product categories, customer segments, and regional trends.

**2. Key Analysis Questions** <br>
* What are the current key business metrics (e.g., sales, profit, and growth), and how are they performing overall?
* How has business performance changed over time, and where are the key growth opportunities?
* How does performance vary across different regions and product categories?
* What are the key customer segments, and how do their purchasing behaviors differ?
